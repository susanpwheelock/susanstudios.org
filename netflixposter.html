Netflix Movie Promotional Art

This Netflix movie promotional artwork is based on the horror genre. I chose to name is Miss Delphine Asaka because Asaka means beautiful death in Japanese, and Delphine means deadly. I wanted the overall feel of this poster to be illusionistic because she is beautiful but harmful.

She is a beautiful woman who uses her beauty to trick other people into getting what she wants while harming them with her witchy magic. The time frame is based on the Victorian era with some Gothic influence.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Year: Spring, 2025
